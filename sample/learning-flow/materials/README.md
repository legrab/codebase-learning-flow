## Rules

- Derive material from canonical research.
- Reference research instead of duplicating its evidence.
- Plan sessions for 30 to 40 minutes by default.
- Offer shorter variants when useful.
- Split long content into several sessions.
- Stay at most one useful session ahead unless deeper research is requested.
- Store generic exercises here.
- Store tailored questions, answers, and follow-up in contributor files.
- Improve shared explanations whenever contributor confusion reveals a reusable gap.
- Validate watched repository locations before providing a new session.
